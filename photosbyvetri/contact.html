Frequently Asked Questions

What is your booking process?

Simply contact us via phone or email, discuss your requirements, and we'll provide you with a customized quote. Once you confirm, we'll secure your date with a booking deposit.

How long does it take to receive photos?

Typically, edited photos are delivered within 2-3 weeks after the shoot. Rush delivery options are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer packages?

Yes! We offer various customizable packages for different photography needs. Contact us to discuss the best option for your budget and requirements.

Can you travel for shoots?

Absolutely! We travel within India and internationally for destination shoots. Travel costs will be included in your quote.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days before the event will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days will forfeit 50% of the booking amount.

Can I request specific styles or poses?

Of course! We encourage clients to share inspiration and preferences. We'll work together to ensure you get exactly what you envision.
