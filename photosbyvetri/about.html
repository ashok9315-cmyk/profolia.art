About Me

Passionate Photographer & Visual Storyteller

Welcome to Photosbyvetri

I'm Vetri, a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My passion for photography began in my teenage years and has evolved into a full-fledged career dedicated to telling stories through the lens.

My journey has taken me through diverse genres of photography - from intimate wedding ceremonies to breathtaking landscapes, from corporate events to artistic portraits. Each project is approached with the same level of dedication and creativity.

I believe that every photograph should evoke emotions and tell a story. My philosophy is simple: capture authentic moments, showcase genuine emotions, and create memories that last a lifetime.
